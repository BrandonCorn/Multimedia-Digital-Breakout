Lisa and Hank are picking up their friend..

Rey from Comic-Con and she needs help finishing her scavenger hunt. Each clue is a riddle and they are on the final riddle. They've found where to look for the last item, a stand full of unique socks, but they need to solve the riddle given to them by the clerk to pick the correct item to win the hunt.

This is a perfect time to explain...

linked lists. Linked lists are linear lists whose order isn't given by their physical placement. Instead, each element points to the next. This scavenger hunt is similar to a linked list because starting with the first riddle for the hunt, you must solve the current riddle to move on to the next, there is no way to skip points of the hunt because you need the second riddle to reach the third and so on until you finish.

Similarly,

linked lists contain nodes where your data is stored and to reach the next node where data is stored you need to start from the beginning and work your way through the connections until you reach your destination.
